Entities

HTML entities are special codes used to represent characters that have specific meanings in HTML, such as `<`, `>`, `&`, and quotes. They are used to display reserved characters, non-printable characters, or characters that cannot be typed on a standard keyboard.

Examples

  1. `&l t;` represents `<`
  2. `&g t;` represents `>`
  3. `&am p;` represents `&`
  4. `&quo t;` represents `"`
  5. `&# 39;` represents `'`
